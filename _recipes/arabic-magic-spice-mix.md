# Arabic Magic Spice Mix

## Ingredients

- 1/2 Cup Allspice
- 1/2 Cup Black Pepper
- 1/2 Cup White Pepper
- 1/2 Cup Cinnamon
- 1/4 Cup Ginger
- 1/4 Cup Cloves
- 1/4 Cup Mace
- 1/4 Cup Nutmeg

## Directions

1. Mix all spices together. Store in airtight container, away from sunlight.
